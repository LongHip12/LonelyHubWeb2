1. Data We Collect

We collect your username, email address, and password hash when you register. We also log session tokens, IP addresses for security purposes, and content you upload or post.

Your data is used to provide and improve the service, send transactional emails (e.g. verification codes), and detect abuse or fraud. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We use secure, HttpOnly cookies to maintain your login session. See our Cookie Policy for details.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Session data expires automatically. We may retain logs for up to 90 days for security purposes.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us on Discord.
