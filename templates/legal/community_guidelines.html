Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Lonely Hub is a community for Minecraft players. To keep it safe and enjoyable for everyone, please follow these guidelines.

Be Respectful
Treat all community members with respect. Personal attacks, harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or threats will result in an immediate ban.
No Malware or Harmful Content
Never upload mods, files, or links that contain viruses, malware, ransomware, or any code intended to harm users' systems.
Respect Intellectual Property
Only upload mods you created or have explicit permission to distribute. Credit original authors. Do not steal or redistribute others' work without permission.
No Spam or Self-Promotion Abuse
Do not flood forums, comments, or messages with spam, repeated posts, or excessive self-promotion.
Consequences
Violations may result in content removal, temporary mute, temporary ban, or permanent ban depending on severity.
